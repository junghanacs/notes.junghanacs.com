````markdown
To enable [Google Analytics](https://marketingplatform.google.com/about/analytics/), set `services.googleAnalytics.ID` flag in `hugo.yaml`:

```yaml {filename="hugo.yaml"}
services:
  googleAnalytics:
    ID: G-MEASUREMENT_ID
```
````
